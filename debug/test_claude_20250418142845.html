FAQ

1. What materials can be engraved using ComMarker's B4 MOPA 100W fiber laser engraving machine?

The B4 MOPA 100W fiber laser engraving machine can engrave a variety of materials including metals like gold, aluminum (with dark color effects), stainless steel (with multiple colors), platinum, silver, titanium, brass, tungsten, carbide, nickel, carbon steel, chrome, copper, iron, and others. It also works well with plastics such as ABS, PC, PLA, silicon, and PBT.

2. Which materials can I cut?

You can cut stainless steel, aluminum, brass, copper, silver, and gold with the B4 MOPA 100W.

3. What is the warranty of this machine?

The B4 MOPA 100W comes with a 2-year warranty.

4. How long does the laser source last in the B4 MOPA 100W fiber laser engraving machine?

The laser source has a lifespan of over 100,000 hours, ensuring long-lasting performance.

5. What software is included with the B4 MOPA 100W laser fiber engraver?

The machine is bundled with EZCad software and is compatible with LightBurn software (not included).

6. How large is the work area provided by the B4 MOPA 100W fiber laser engraving machine?

The B4 MOPA 100W offers a work area of 4.3×4.3 inches (110×110 mm) and 7.8×7.8 inches (200×200 mm). Additional quartz lenses ranging from 70mm to 300mm can be ordered if necessary.
